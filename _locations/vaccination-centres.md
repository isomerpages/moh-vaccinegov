Locations that are administering the Covid-19 Vaccines. 


Use [Map](https://www.onemap.sg/main/v2/vaccination) to find a Vaccination Centre near you.

**Normal Operating Hours:**<br>
8am – 10pm Daily<br>
8am – 3pm on Eve of Public Holidays

**Breaks:**<br>
11am – 12pm,<br>
5pm – 6pm

 **Last Walk-in**:<br>
 7pm daily<br>
 1pm on eve of Public Holiday<br><br>
 

**For VCs ceasing operations on 30 June 2022**:
* VCs offering the Moderna/Spikevax vaccine will administer the last Dose 1 on 2 June 2022.<br>
* VCs offering the Pfizer-BioNTech/Comirnaty vaccine will administer the last Dose 1 on 9 June
2022.<br>
* Dose 2 and booster doses will continue to be administered until 30 June 2022.<br>

<br>
**Upcoming Public Holidays Operating Hours**<br>Please note that the last appointment on the eve of the Public Holiday will be at 2pm.<br>

10 Jul 2022 (Hari Raya Haji) - Closed<br>
8 Aug 2022 (Eve of National Day): 8am to 3pm<br>
9 Aug 2022 (National Day) - Closed<br>
23 Oct 2022 (Eve of Deepavali): 8am to 3pm<br>
24 Oct 2022 (Deepavali) - Closed<br>
24 Dec 2022 (Christmas Day's Eve): 8am to 3pm<br>
